Signal and Image Processing Lab

Professor : 吳俊霖
Location : Applied Technology Building, Room 347 [Tel: 04-22840884 ext. 347]
Website : https://drphototw.wixsite.com/wujl/siplab
Introduction : The SIP Lab focuses on image processing, machine learning, machine vision, multimedia signal processing, image recognition, computational photography, and other related fields. In addition to research on related theories and algorithms, we also actively participate in related industry-academia cooperation projects, hoping to contribute to the technological improvement of related industries through cross-disciplinary cooperation. At present, our main research directions are: 1) image processing and machine vision, including image processing and deblurring techniques, camera calibration and optical defect detection applications; 2) machine learning, including theories and applications of deep learning; 3) multimedia signal processing, including audio fingerprinting and video processing; 4) image recognition, including statistical and neural network-like classifier design; 5) computational photography; 6) computational photography. Computational photography; 6. High-dimensional image data analysis.

Medical Image Processing Lab

Professor : 喻石生
Location : Room 1022A, Science Building [Tel: 04-22840497 ext. 622]
Website : http://miplab.cs.nchu.edu.tw/
Introduction : Our laboratory is mainly engaged in the research of medical image processing and recognition. From medical image processing to feature acquisition, graphic recognition and statistical analysis to build an expert system. Our research focuses on training students with the ability of medical image analysis and processing, image acquisition, image watermarking and image encryption to achieve the purpose of image recognition. At present, our main research directions are: a. Medical image processing and identification b. Image security c. Bioinformatics d. Automated machine applications

VLSI/CAD Lab

Professor : 王行健
Location : Room 922A, Science Building [Tel: 04-22840497 ext. 922]
Website : http://vlsilab.cs.nchu.edu.tw/
Introduction : This lab is mainly engaged in the research and development of VLSI design, verification, testing, and design automation software technologies. The main research areas include low power design, low power testing, test data compression, built-in self-testing techniques, scan testing, delay testing, and physical winding. At present, our main research areas are: a. Ultra-large scale integrated circuit design: circuit and logic design, FPGA design, fault tolerant system, low power circuit design. Verification: compliance verification, functional verification, and time verification. Digital circuit / system single chip test: test data compression, low power test, built-in self-test, testable design, memory test. Computer-aided design: High-level synthesis, logic synthesis, physical synthesis.

Graphics, Multimedia & Virtual Reality Lab

Professor : 王宗銘
Location : Room 904, Science Building [Tel: 04-22840497 ext. 904]
Website :
Introduction : The laboratory studies issues related to the fields of computer graphics, multimedia, and virtual reality. The results of our research can be applied to image processing, production of computer games or animations, secret communication, and protection of intellectual property rights of 3D models. Currently, our main research directions are: i. Image color group: research on grayscale image coloring techniques, use of color shift to change the color of target images, Chinese ink painting simulation; ii. Material texture group: research on material synthesis, material transfer, natural cloud simulation; iii. 3D model group: research on 2D vector diagrams, 3D polyhedra, 3D point model watermarking and information hiding.

Computer Vision & Multimedia Lab

Location : Room 702C, Science Building [Tel: 04-22840497 ext. 716]

Natural Language Processing Lab

Professor : 范耀中
Location : Science Building, Room 721 [Tel: 04-22840497 ext. 721]
Website : http://nlp.cs.nchu.edu.tw
Introduction : Our laboratory focuses on the field of data mining technology; we use data engineering technology as the starting point to consider various kinds of data generated in different applications for query processing and exploration of that type of data.
